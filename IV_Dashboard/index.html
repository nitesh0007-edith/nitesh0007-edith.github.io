Analytical Tasks

  • T1 — Identify the season or month with the highest average temperature range.
  • T2 — Find the top 3 weather types by frequency across the dataset.
  • T3 — Determine which year had the highest average wind speed.
  • T4 — Find the weather type with highest average visibility, filtered by minimum humidity.
  • T5 — Identify months with wind speed and humidity above user-set thresholds. Find the top 3 seasons with the highest average cloud cover.
